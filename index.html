Elizabeth Chlarson | WDD 130

I am from central Illinois, where I live with my wonderful husband and our two children (6 and 3). Durring our almost twelve years of marriage we have lived in eight different areas, all located in central Illinois. Though I have worked at a few places, most recently I worked at State Farm's Corporate Office. Currently I am a stay-at-home mom while I am working on my Bachelors in Applied Technology, with about a year and a half until graduation. When I am not studying, I keep myself busy these days with my two kids, three callings, and lots of sewing.

In addition to my love for technology, I am an avid quilter. Every spare minuite I have is spent sitting at a sewing machine or with my noes in a book. Though I can crochet, knit, embroider, paint, and much more; my passion has always been to quilt. There is something special about a quilt that brings comfort and love to those they are gifted to.
